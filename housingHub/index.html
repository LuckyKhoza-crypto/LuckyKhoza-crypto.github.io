The goal of our project was to create a way for all students to have easy, equal, and affordable access to off campus housing and off campus housing information. We recognize that around the country, there is an inequality for housing opportunity, whether it's in the form of affordable housing, location of housing, or even knowing the state's laws for tenants and real estate agents. We had a goal of making all that information clear and concise, and in one easy location for all students who are looking for off campus housing, or simply want to learn more about housing laws and regulations when it comes to renting and buying houses.
